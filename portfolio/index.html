"Learn to create, create to learn."



Who I am:

Hey friends! I am Ronda. I am an educator turned software developer. After teaching in the classroom for 8 years I realized how interested I was in technology and decided to pursue a career as a full-stack developer.

When I first started teaching my classroom had a couple of “antique” IMACs in a beautiful Indigo color for students to use, but that was it as far as technology. Now it’s very normal for most classrooms to have a full set of iPads for the students. I’ve seen how wonderful a new educational app can be to a student struggling to learn how to read. I loved learning about apps as they came out and figuring out how to best implement them in the classroom.

How I got here:

I think my love for the internet and educational apps motivated me to study software development. I enjoy designing from scratch and creating something unique. Currently I am studying at Digital Crafts, and so far it's been a great experience.

What I am up to now:

I am working on a couple ideas for educational apps. One is a web app to teach elementary students the fundamentals of poetry and how to put words together to create something beautiful. It will be interactive and the goal is to have the students write a poem with guidance from the app.
